Fitting In – Nelson’s County

Particularly for those of you who have limited experience of the driest county in the UK (tempting fate much!), we wanted to give you a few handy phrases (phonetically) to help you fit in “loike” a local.

  • “Up city” – I am going into Norwich
  • “yer orrite boi?” – Good morning, how are you doing?
  • “ass a rummun” – That sounds a little out of the ordinary
  • “Bootiful” – beautiful
  • “Slow you down” – Slow down
  • “Ass on the huh” – Do you have a spirit level
